# Glia Privacy Policy

Glia does not collect, transmit, store remotely, or share any data.

Your knowledge graph is read from a folder you choose on your Mac and never
leaves it. The Mac App Store build makes no network requests. There are no
analytics, no accounts, no telemetry, and no third-party services.

Spotlight indexing (optional, on-device) uses Apple's CoreSpotlight and
stays local to your Mac.

Questions: open an issue on the repository.
